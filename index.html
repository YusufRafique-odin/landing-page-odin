The World of Elephants

Elephants are the largest land mammals on earth and have distinctly massive bodies, large ears, and long trunks. They are the gentle giants of the animal kingdom. At Elephant's Lover Society we strive to create a community for everything related to elephants ranging from conservation, educational articals and cool photography.

Fascinating facts about elephants

A newborn elephant can stand up shortly after being born. They can weigh up to 260 pounds.

The average life span for an elephant in the wild is from 50 to 70 years. They oldest known elephant in the world lived to be 82 years of age.

The trunk of an elephant has more than 150,000 muscles and tendons in it.

Elephants can paint!

I can watch elephants (and elephants alone) for hours at a time, for sooner or later the elephant will do something very strange... There is mystery behind that masked gray visage, and ancient life force, delicate and mighty, awesome and enchanted, commanding the silence ordinarily reserved for mountain peaks, great fires, and the sea.
